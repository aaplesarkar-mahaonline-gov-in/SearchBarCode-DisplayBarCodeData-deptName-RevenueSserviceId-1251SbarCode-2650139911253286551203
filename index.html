  • Quick Service

    Quick Service

    • Delivery of public services has now become quicker. Notified services will be delivered in a stipulated time period. Right to appeal in case of delay or wrongful rejection of service.
    • You don't even require to attend such 2-3 queues and visit the office regularly to obtain required certificate.
    • Just visit our portal, understand the requirements for desired certificate, visit service center with required documents and fees. Operator will fill up respective form online and you will get the receipt for the same. Desired certificate will be delivered at your doorstep within a specific timeframe.
  • Service At Doorstep

    Service At Doorstep

    • Avoid long queues to avail public services. Apply for various services through ‘Aaple Sarkar’, an online portal at your convenience.
    • Visit the center and submit the documents. It's simple!!!
  • Easy Access

    Easy Access

    • Single window for services across departments. The appeals can also be filed online.
    • Search nearest center in desired area on single click and visit the center.
    • You can also fill up the forms online for several services on your own.
  • Easy Payment

    Easy Payment

    • Easy online payment for availing the service with safe and secure payment gateway.
    • You can simply pay through Net banking.
  • User Friendly

    User Friendly

    • Easy to use, hassle free, information technology based solution for application, uploading attachments, tracking status of application, etc.
    • Our operators and system, both are user friendly, which ensures a customer friendly relation with you.
  • Save Time

    Save Time

    • Save time and money by applying online with minimal paper work. No need to visit government office for follow-up.
    • Visit our center, complete the procedure and the certificate/extract will be delivered at your doorstep within a certain time frame.
